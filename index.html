Welcome to the Coliseum, magister!

Your three champions will do their best to ensure your triumph against Veknos' goons!

Do keep in mind that while they are all great fighters, they all have their own strengths and weaknesses.

Warrior excels against rogues, but suffer against mages; rogues are great at taking down wizards but stand no chance when facing a warrior; and while wizards may handily dispatch warriors, they will quickly be brought down by a rogue.

The challenge will proceed as follows: each turn, you will select one of your champions to enter the arena, as will Veknos; the first team to reach 5 victories will be declared the winner.

But enough talk! Let's get on with the games!
