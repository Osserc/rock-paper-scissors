Welcome to the Coliseum, magister!

Your three champions will do their best to defend your honor and ensure your triumph over Veknos' goons!

Do keep in mind that while they are all great fighters, they all have their own strengths and weaknesses.

Warriors excel against rogues, but suffer against wizards; rogues are great at taking down wizards but stand no chance when facing a warrior; and while wizards may handily dispatch warriors, they will quickly be brought down by a rogue.

The challenge will proceed as follows: each turn, you will select one of your champions to the arena, as will Veknos; the first team to reach 5 victories will be declared the winner.

But enough talk! Let's get on with the trials!
